About The Coop

We are a poultry restaurant based in the heart of galway. Our aim has always been to deliver high quality dishes using local Artisan and farmers produce to support our local economy. Our Poultry is ethically sourced and is free range. We support the local farmers markets and change our restaurants menu with seasons. We provide elegant food but in a casual environment. Good food in a calm relaxing environment!
